Our Story

Anh Duong Melody was established in 2020 with the intention of bringing international style makeup and cosmetics to the Vietnam market. Our technicians are well trained in cosemetic techniques adopted internationally, in particular Japan and Korea.

We specialize in making you look beautiful. Our core capabilities include but is not limitted to make up for events, wedding makeup, cosmetic eyebrow tattoo, cosmetic lip tattoo. In addition to making you beautiful, we sell cosmetic products from the beloved USA!
